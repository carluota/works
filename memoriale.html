2025 Milano Il Memoriale del Vuoto Archive design
An archive that reimagines Scalo Farini—a former railway yard in Milan—as a space of identity and narrative rather than absence. It explores how subjective perceptions and sensory impressions can transform overlooked urban voids into sites of meaning, memory, and reflection.
With G. Bernasconi, G. Cantarutti, N. Patitucci, G. Pulcini
